David McMorris, Ph.D.

  • CV

I earned my PhD in mathematics at the University of Nebraska - Lincoln under the advisement of Dr. Glenn Ledder. My research focuses on problems in mathematical biology and optimal control theory.  I earned my BS in mathematics from Hope College and my MS in mathematics from UNL.  Outside of mathematics I enjoy running, cycling, and lots of coffee.

Research

I am primarily interested in mathematical biology with a current focus on applications of control theory to plant life history and ecology. I am also interested in scientific computing and computational fluid dynamics.
